Recipe for Spaghetti

Spaghetti Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Ground Beef
  • Spaghetti Pasta (Homemade of Store-bought)
  • Parmesan

Instructions

  1. Cut all vegetables finely
  2. Puree vegetables into a sauce
  3. Preheat a pan to high
  4. Boil water in a stockpot
  5. One water is boiling, add spaghetti pasta to the water and cook until soft
  6. Add sauce to pan and bring to low boil
  7. Add ground beef to the pan and cook until brown
  8. Add cooked pasta to the pan and stir until sauce is well-distributed
